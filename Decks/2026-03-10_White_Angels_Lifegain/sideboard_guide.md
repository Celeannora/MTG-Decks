# Sideboard Guide: White Angels Lifegain

## vs. Aggro Red / Burn

**Boarding:**
- Out: 3x Savior of the Sleeping (too slow), 1x Serra Angel (too expensive)
- In: 3x Sanctuary Wall (blocks and stuns), 3x Savior of the Small (generates blocker tokens on survival)

**Gameplan:** Stabilize early with Sanguine Syphoner lifelink and Sanctuary Wall's tap ability. Let Pridemate grow via the life gain. Win through a fat Pridemate or Serra Angel once stable.

**Key Cards:** Sanguine Syphoner (drains them), Sanctuary Wall (buys time), Ajani's Pridemate (grows large fast off lifegain)

**Critical Turns:** Turn 2–3 are make or break. Surviving to turn 4 with any lifegain source active is a near-win.

---

## vs. Control (Blue/White Tempo)

**Boarding:**
- Out: 2x Savannah Lions (dies to every interaction), 1x Sanguine Syphoner (slow vs. control)
- In: 3x Selfless Police Captain (resilient 2-drop that passes counters on death), 3x Scrawling Crawler (forces them to answer or face grinding chip damage)

**Gameplan:** Use Anafenza's flash to bait removal on your own terms. Angel of Finality on ETB hits their graveyard, cutting recursion. Aim to resolve Serra Angel under open mana windows.

**Key Cards:** Anafenza, Unyielding Lineage (flash + first strike dodges sorcery removal), Angel of Finality (hoses graveyard recursion)

**Critical Turns:** Turn 5–6. Control decks want to land a finisher after countering your threats. Get to 6 mana before them.

---

## vs. Green Midrange / Stompy

**Boarding:**
- Out: 2x Sanguine Syphoner (too small), 1x Savannah Lions (outclassed in ground combat)
- In: 3x Scrapshooter (destroys key enchantments/artifacts), 2x Selfless Police Captain (sticky threat)

**Gameplan:** Go over the top with flying from Serra Angel and Sage of the Skies. Seraphic Steed generating 3/3 Angel tokens is key — they can't block flying and ground threats simultaneously.

**Key Cards:** Serra Angel (vigilance blocks their biggest ground creature), Seraphic Steed (creates aerial army)

**Critical Turns:** Turn 4+. Green decks run out of gas around turn 6–7; outlast them.

---

## vs. Reanimator / Graveyard

**Boarding:**
- Out: 3x Savior of the Sleeping (sideboard upgrade), 1x Seraphic Steed (not needed here)
- In: 3x Scrawling Crawler (attrition piece), 3x Sanctuary Wall (time-buying blocker)

**Gameplan:** Angel of Finality's ETB destroys their graveyard setup. Prioritize casting it over any other 4-drop. Play aggressively and close before they can establish recursion loops.

**Key Cards:** Angel of Finality (direct graveyard hate), Anafenza, Unyielding Lineage (creates spirits from dying creatures, hates on graveyard play)

**Critical Turns:** Turn 4 (Angel of Finality ETB). Must resolve cleanly.

---

## vs. Token Swarm

**Boarding:**
- Out: 3x Aerith Gainsborough (dies to 1/1 token pings), 1x Sanguine Syphoner
- In: 3x Sanctuary Wall (taps down attackers), 2x Scrapshooter (destroys token-generating enchantments/artifacts)

**Gameplan:** Serra Angel's vigilance allows attacking while still blocking their tokens. Anafenza's first strike and deathtouch-adjacent endure provides excellent blocking. Win by grinding through their token generation with flyers.

**Key Cards:** Serra Angel (vigilance is key), Anafenza, Unyielding Lineage (first strike demolishes 1/1 tokens), Sanctuary Wall (stuns their best attacker)

**Critical Turns:** Turn 3–4. If they establish a wide board before your flyers arrive, it's difficult. Prioritize Anafenza early.
