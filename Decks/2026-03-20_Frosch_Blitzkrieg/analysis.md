## Executive Summary

**Deck name:** Frosch Blitzkrieg ("Frog Lightning War")
**Colors:** Simic (Green/Blue)
**Format:** Standard
**Archetype:** Frog Tribal Aggro-Midrange

### The German History Theme

The deck mirrors three pillars of German military doctrine:

| Doctrine | MTG Equivalent |
|----------|----------------|
| **Blitzkrieg** — overwhelming speed, strike before the enemy can respond | 1-drop Frog Warriors flood the board before turn 3 |
| **Stosstruppen** — elite shock troops who probe for weaknesses | Deathtouch frogs (Poison Dart Frog, Frog Butler) that trade up |
| **Bewegungskrieg** — war of movement, never stay static | Splash Portal + Mistbreath Elder bounce frogs back to hand to re-enter and re-trigger effects |
| **Generalstab** — general staff / high command | Clement, Glarb, Genghis Frog as legendary commanders of the army |
| **Siegetaktik** — siege warfare | Glacierwood Siege represents the siege: lock down card advantage and mill |

### Primary Win Condition

**Combat damage via a wide Frog army pumped by Valley Mightcaller.** By turn 4-5, the board is 8-12 power across 5-7 frogs, all with trample on Valley Mightcaller. Polliwallop (Affinity for Frogs) functions as free combat removal. Valley Floodcaller untaps the entire army after a noncreature spell.

**Target win turn:** Turn 5-6 in goldfish; Turn 6-7 against interaction.

## Card-by-Card Breakdown

### Die Stosstruppen (1-drops)

**Valley Mightcaller** (4x) — {G} | Creature — Frog Warrior | Trample
The Wehrmacht's frontline sergeant. Gets a +1/+1 counter every time any Frog, Rabbit, Raccoon, or Squirrel enters. With 4+ frogs entering across turns 1-2, this becomes a 5/5 trampler by turn 3. The soul of the deck.

**Sunshower Druid** (4x) — {G} | Creature — Frog Druid
ETB: puts a +1/+1 counter on target creature + 1 life. Double purpose: pumps Valley Mightcaller on entry AND stabilizes life total against aggressive decks. The field medic of the regiment.

**Mistbreath Elder** (2x) — {G} | Creature — Frog Warrior | Rare
The Stoßtrupp squad leader. At each upkeep, bounces another creature back to hand, growing itself. Enables ETB re-trigger loops with Sunshower Druid, Three Tree Scribe, and Clement. Two copies — powerful but self-limiting since multiples compete for the bounce slot.

### Vanguard — Advance Guard (2-drops)

**Frog Butler** (4x) — {1}{G} | Creature — Frog Spirit | Deathtouch
The poisoned dart from the marshes. Taps for any color mana (critical for splashing), threatens deathtouch combat, and can grow reach. Named "Butler" but fights like a panzergrenadier.

**Poison Dart Frog** (3x) — {1}{G} | Creature — Frog | Reach + Conditional Deathtouch
The jungle warfare specialist. Taps for any color mana, blocks fliers via Reach, and pays {2} to gain deathtouch. Three copies because 4 deathtouch mana-dorks is too much; this creates enough threat density at the 2-drop slot.

**Three Tree Scribe** (2x) — {1}{G} | Creature — Frog Druid
Whenever a creature leaves without dying (bounce, Splash Portal, Mistbreath Elder), this puts a +1/+1 counter on target creature. Combos tightly with Mistbreath Elder and Clement's bounce. The intelligence officer who grows stronger from every maneuver.

**Dour Port-Mage** (1x) — {1}{U} | Creature — Frog Wizard | Rare
Draws a card when creatures leave without dying. Acts as a singleton card draw engine in the bounce-and-recast loop. One copy because it's legendary-adjacent in power density; two would flood.

**Genghis Frog** (2x) — {G}{U} | Legendary Creature — Frog Mutant Rogue | Trample
Named after the greatest conqueror in history — fitting for a deck themed around military domination. Creates Mutagen tokens when Mutants enter (frogs in the TMT set are Mutants). Trample pushes combat damage through blockers.

### Hauptkampflinie — Main Battle Line (3-drops)

**Clement, the Worrywort** (2x) — {1}{G}{U} | Legendary Creature — Frog Druid | Vigilance | Rare
The Feldmarschall. Gives ALL frogs the ability to tap for {G}/{U} to cast creature spells. Bounces a lesser creature when any creature enters, enabling the ETB re-trigger chain. Two copies: legendary, but the second is always useful in hand as a target for Clement's own bounce.

**Long River Lurker** (1x) — {2}{U} | Creature — Frog Scout | Ward {1}
Grants all other frogs Ward {1}. Also makes one creature unblockable for the turn — a direct analog to German infiltration tactics. One copy as a silver bullet.

**Waterspout Warden** (1x) — {2}{U} | Creature — Frog Soldier
When it attacks, if another creature entered this turn, it gains flying. The aerial vanguard of the assault. One copy at the top of the 3-drop range.

### Oberkommando — High Command (4-drops)

**Valley Floodcaller** (1x) — {2}{U} | Creature — Otter Wizard | Flash | Rare
The Blitzkrieg enabler. Cast a noncreature spell → untap ALL Birds, Frogs, Otters, Rats + pump +1/+1. Cast Polliwallop mid-combat, untap your entire frog army, and suddenly you have double blocks or a second wave of attacks. The Panzer breakthrough in card form.

**Dreamdew Entrancer** (1x) — {2}{G}{U} | Creature — Frog Wizard | Reach | Rare
Taps a creature and puts 3 stun counters on it upon entry — effectively removing an enemy threat for 3 turns. If you tap your own creature, draws 2 cards. The Wehrmacht's prisoner interrogation unit (with paperwork).

### Kriegsdoktrin — War Doctrine (Enchantments)

**Glacierwood Siege** (2x) — {1}{G}{U} | Enchantment — Temur/Sultai Mode
Choose Sultai: you may play lands from your graveyard + cast spells from your graveyard for their mana cost. This represents the long siege: deny the enemy resources while replenishing your own. In a deck bouncing frogs every turn, graveyard recursion adds a second line of attack.

**Innkeeper's Talent** (2x) — {1}{G} | Enchantment — Class
Level 1: puts +1/+1 counter on a creature at start of combat each turn. Level 2: permanents you control have +1/+1. The military drill instructor — constant discipline makes the army stronger. Pumps Valley Mightcaller to threatening levels by turn 3.

**Gossip's Talent** (1x) — {1}{U} | Enchantment — Class
Level 1: surveil 1 on each creature ETB. Synergizes perfectly with the high-ETB frequency of this deck. The intelligence network: every new recruit brings information from the front lines.

### Blitzangriff — Instants / Sorceries

**Polliwallop** (3x) — {3}{G} | Instant | Affinity for Frogs
With 4+ frogs in play, this costs {G} or even {0}. Target creature deals 2× its power to another creature — removes virtually anything. The Stuka dive bomber: devastating and surprisingly cheap.

**Synchronized Charge** (1x) — {1}{G} | Sorcery
Distributes 2 counters, then all creatures with counters gain vigilance + trample. One card that turns the entire board into a trample army. The coordinated Wehrmacht advance.

**Splash Portal** (1x) — {U} | Sorcery
Exile a creature, return it at end step. If it's a Frog, draw a card. Dodges removal, re-triggers ETBs, and draws a card — all for {U}. The Wehrmacht's deceptive misdirection tactics.

## Weaknesses and Mitigations

1. **Sweepers (Day of Judgment effects):** Vulnerability to mass removal. Mitigation: sideboard in Stickytongue Sentinel to bounce key pieces before sweeper resolves.
2. **Graveyard hate:** Glacierwood Siege's Sultai mode becomes weaker vs. Leyline of the Void effects. Primary plan unaffected since deck is creature-first.
3. **Counterspell-heavy blue decks:** Ward frogs (Long River Lurker) + Clement's mana generation make taxing difficult. Post-board: bring in Burrog Befuddler's flash to play around counterspell timing.
4. **Lack of flying defense:** Waterspout Warden conditionally flies. Sideboard Clifftop Lookout (Reach, instant-speed land tutor) shores up the air.

The deck operates in three modes:
- **Blitzkrieg Mode** (fastest): Turn 1 Valley Mightcaller, Turn 2 two frogs → VM is 3/3 trample, attack for 3. This continues to scale.

- **Siegetaktik Mode** (late game): Glacierwood Siege Sultai keeps the gas flowing from the graveyard; Dreamdew Entrancer locks down their best creature for 3 turns.
