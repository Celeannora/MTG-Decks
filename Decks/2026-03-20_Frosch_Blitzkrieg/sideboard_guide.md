# Frosch Blitzkrieg — Sideboard Guide

## Sideboard Overview

| Card | Count | Purpose |
|------|-------|---------|
| Stickytongue Sentinel | 3 | Creature bounce protection vs. sweepers; reach blocker |
| Burrog Befuddler | 3 | Flash frog vs. control; ETB -1/-0 debuff on entry |
| Clifftop Lookout | 2 | Ramp + reach blocker vs. flying aggro; instant-speed land |
| Gossip's Talent | 2 | More card filtering vs. midrange/control (up to 3 total) |
| Glacierwood Siege | 2 | Extra graveyard recursion vs. grindy matchups (up to 4 total) |
| Synchronized Charge | 2 | More pump vs. creature-heavy decks; vigilance + trample mass grant |
| Dour Port-Mage | 1 | Second copy for bounce-loop value vs. control |

---

## Matchup Plans

### vs. Aggro / Red Burn

**Strategy:** Stabilize with deathtouch blocks. Gain life with Sunshower Druid.

**In:**
- +3 Stickytongue Sentinel (more bodies, bounce key pieces before lethal)
- +2 Clifftop Lookout (Reach to block fliers)

**Out:**
- -1 Glacierwood Siege (too slow)
- -1 Gossip's Talent (too slow)
- -1 Splash Portal (cute but not necessary)
- -1 Synchronized Charge
- -1 Dreamdew Entrancer

---

### vs. Control (Counterspell heavy)

**Strategy:** Flood the board with low-CMC frogs. Ward protection makes individual counters ineffective. Use Clement's mana to regenerate threats.

**In:**
- +3 Burrog Befuddler (Flash frog, dodge sorcery-speed sweepers; ETB disrupts their key creatures)
- +1 Dour Port-Mage (more draw engine in the bounce loop)
- +2 Gossip's Talent (more filtering to find threats)

**Out:**
- -1 Waterspout Warden (conditional flyer not needed)
- -2 Glacierwood Siege (they answer enchantments)
- -1 Polliwallop (they have fewer big creatures)
- -1 Synchronized Charge
- -1 Sunshower Druid (gain life less relevant)

---

### vs. Midrange / Big Creatures

**Strategy:** Use Polliwallop + deathtouch to win combat. Dreamdew Entrancer stuns their biggest threat for 3 turns.

**In:**
- +2 Synchronized Charge (mass trample enables racing)
- +2 Clifftop Lookout (ramp to 4-drops faster)

**Out:**
- -1 Gossip's Talent
- -1 Splash Portal
- -1 Long River Lurker (Ward less important vs. creature decks)
- -1 Waterspout Warden

---

### vs. Graveyard / Reanimator

**Strategy:** Their plan takes time. Blitzkrieg them before they set up. Glacierwood Siege's Sultai mode mirrors their graveyard advantage.

**In:**
- +2 Glacierwood Siege (maximum Sultai mode value; play lands from graveyard)
- +2 Synchronized Charge (end the game faster)

**Out:**
- -1 Gossip's Talent
- -1 Waterspout Warden
- -1 Splash Portal
- -1 Dour Port-Mage (bounce loop less needed when racing)

---

### vs. Tokens / Wide Boards

**Strategy:** Polliwallop their biggest token. Valley Floodcaller untaps all frogs after a spell to maximize blockers/attackers.

**In:**
- +3 Stickytongue Sentinel (bounce key tokens, re-enter triggers)
- +2 Synchronized Charge (trample + vigilance lets you attack AND block)

**Out:**
- -2 Glacierwood Siege (too slow in racing matchup)
- -1 Long River Lurker
- -1 Gossip's Talent
- -1 Waterspout Warden
