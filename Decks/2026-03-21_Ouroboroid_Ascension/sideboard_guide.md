# Sideboard Guide — Ouroboroid Ascension

## Sideboard

| Card | Count | Purpose |
|------|-------|---------|
| Disdainful Stroke | 3 | Counter CMC 4+ (sweepers, ramp payoffs, control finishers) |
| Blossoming Defense | 3 | Hexproof + +2/+2 vs. exile removal and targeted interaction |
| Confounding Riddle | 2 | Counter CMC 4+ OR loot 4; flexible two-mode spell |
| Synchronized Charge | 2 | Distribute 2 counters + vigilance/trample vs. midrange races |
| Snakeskin Veil | 2 | {G} hexproof + +1/+1 counter (doubled with DS); cheapest protection |
| Case of the Trampled Garden | 2 | Additional counter distribution + trample enabler when solved |
| Tyvar, the Pummeler | 1 | Fourth copy effectively; brings extra closing punch vs. slower decks |

---

## Matchup Plans

### vs. Red Aggro / Mono-Red Burn

**Plan:** District Mascot is a 2/2 blocker for {G}. Bristly Bill turns every land into a bigger blocker. Inspiring Call makes everything indestructible to survive sweepers. We grow faster than they kill.

**Board Out:**
- −2 Doubling Season *(4 mana on the defensive is too slow; we need early presence)*
- −1 Goldvein Hydra *(haste is good but we need survival over explosiveness)*
- −1 Deepfathom Echo *(4-mana copy effect isn't where we want to be vs. burn)*
- −1 Case of the Trampled Garden *(too slow at 3 mana when we're stabilizing)*

**Board In:**
- +3 Blossoming Defense *(hexproof + +2/+2 protects Bristly Bill from Lightning Strike)*
- +2 Snakeskin Veil *({G} hexproof saves Ouroboroid from a burn spell right before combat trigger)*

**Key notes:** Mossborn Hydra on T3 becomes a 4-8 toughness trample blocker quickly. Their burn can't kill a creature with 8+ toughness. Biosynthic Burst's indestructible at instant speed saves anything at {1}{G}.

---

### vs. Blue Control (Azorius/Dimir/Esper)

**Plan:** Repulsive Mutation punishes every counterspell they tap out for. Deepfathom Echo copies Ouroboroid for a second trigger. Race through their interaction with a critical mass of threats.

**Board Out:**
- −2 Bioengineered Future *(they're not attacking your board; the landfall bonus matters less)*
- −1 Tyvar, the Pummeler *(expensive; they'll counter it anyway in the early turns)*
- −1 Case of the Trampled Garden *(win-more vs. control)*

**Board In:**
- +2 Confounding Riddle *(counter their wipes OR loot for key cards)*
- +2 Disdainful Stroke *(hit their 4+ CMC answers, counter your stuff back)*

**Key notes:** Repulsive Mutation on a 6-power Mossborn Hydra means "counter target spell unless they pay 6" for {1}{G}{U}. Hold this mana open every turn — it punishes tapping out for sweepers. Jadelight Spelunker cast for X=3 filters land vs. non-land to improve draws.

---

### vs. Midrange (Golgari/Jund/Domain-lite)

**Plan:** Outscale them on counter accumulation. Repulsive Mutation counters their removal while growing the targeted creature. Synchronized Charge gives trample to push through their large blockers.

**Board Out:**
- −1 Deepfathom Echo *(single-threat value isn't as relevant here)*
- −1 Goldvein Hydra *(can stay; cut if they have enough removal for it)*
- −1 Tyvar, the Pummeler *(legendary; one less copy is safe against non-control)*
- −1 Biosynthic Burst *(can stay; cut against removal-light versions)*

**Board In:**
- +2 Synchronized Charge *(two +1/+1 counters to distribute + trample on counter-bearing creatures)*
- +2 Case of the Trampled Garden *(solving at 8 total power is trivial by T3; trample on attackers)*

**Key notes:** This is your best matchup. Their removal answers one creature at a time; Ouroboroid grows every creature simultaneously. By turn 5, they cannot block profitably — everything has 8+ power and trample.

---

### vs. Ramp / Domain / Big Green

**Plan:** Race the ramp with early counter explosions; Disdainful Stroke counters their big payoffs; Tyvar kills before they reach their mana threshold.

**Board Out:**
- −2 Bioengineered Future *(ramp mirror; the bonus counters matter less when we need speed)*
- −1 Case of the Trampled Garden *(win-more in a speed race)*
- −1 Deepfathom Echo *(4 mana in a race)*
- −1 Goldvein Hydra *(treasures irrelevant if we're winning the race)*

**Board In:**
- +3 Disdainful Stroke *(counter Atraxa, Sunfall, Cultivate, any CMC 4+ ramp piece)*
- +2 Confounding Riddle *(two-mode: counter their bomb OR loot into our next threat)*

**Key notes:** Mossborn Hydra on T3 that doubles counters every land is often a bigger threat than their ramp payoff on turn 5-6. Repulsive Mutation holds up threat: "resolve this creature or I counter your next ramp spell."

---

## General Principles

1. **Never board out all Repulsive Mutations.** It's the interactive core of the deck in all matchups.
2. **Inspiring Call stays in every matchup** except pure aggro races where the mana is too tight.
3. **Doubling Season comes out vs. aggro** (too slow to play on curve) but stays in vs. everything slower.
4. **Bristly Bill is always a 4-of** — do not side him out. He is the engine.
5. **Don't activate Bristly Bill's doubling ability** until Ouroboroid has already added counters that turn. Stack order: Ouroboroid trigger resolves → counters doubled by Doubling Season → then activate Bristly Bill to double all of those again.
