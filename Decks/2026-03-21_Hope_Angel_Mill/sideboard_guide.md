# Sideboard Guide — Hope Angel Mill

## Sideboard Composition

| Card | Count | Purpose |
|------|-------|---------|
| Prayer of Binding | 3 | Flash removal + exile + 2 life gain |
| Fumigate | 2 | Board wipe that gains life per creature killed |
| Vanguard Seraph | 2 | Surveil 1 on each life gain event (vs. midrange/control) |
| Temporal Cleansing | 2 | Convoke bounce vs. big single threats |
| Enter the Avatar State | 2 | {W} protection + flying/lifelink/hexproof on a key creature |
| Case of the Uneaten Feast | 2 | Additional lifegain engine (graveyard recursion when solved) |
| Spell Snare | 2 | Counter 2-drops (hits most format staples) |

---

## Matchup Plans

### vs. Red Aggro / Mono-Red Burn

**Plan:** Survive, gain life, stabilize, win.

**Board Out:**
- −2 Space-Time Anomaly *(too slow at sorcery speed; you can't afford turn 4 tapped out)*
- −2 Syncopate *(burn doesn't have spells you need to tax at X; No More Lies is sufficient)*
- −1 Lifecreed Duo *(too small a body for the pace)*

**Board In:**
- +2 Fumigate *(resets their board and gains life = massive swing)*
- +2 Prayer of Binding *(flash at instant speed on their key threats; gains 2 life)*
- +1 Enter the Avatar State *(hexproof Hope Estheim through bolt effects)*

**Key notes:** Authority of the Consuls on T1 is the best possible opener — their creatures entering tapped buys 2 full turns. Ajani's Pridemate grows fast as they cast spells. Don't tap out on your turn against burn.

---

### vs. Green Stompy / Mono-Green Ramp

**Plan:** No More Lies their key ramp targets, establish fliers, close with mill.

**Board Out:**
- −2 Authority of the Consuls *(their creatures aren't ETB-dependent; less impactful)*
- −2 Sheltered by Ghosts *(they have very large blockers; aura is fragile)*

**Board In:**
- +2 Temporal Cleansing *(convoke off your small Angels to bounce their big threat)*
- +2 Vanguard Seraph *(surveil filters; flying blocker is relevant vs. trample attacks)*

**Key notes:** Cavern of Souls naming "Angel" means your main threats can't be countered by their Boseiju effects. Focus on establishing a flying board that they can't attack through.

---

### vs. Blue Control (Azorius/Dimir)

**Plan:** Make Angels uncounterable, resolve threats through interaction, win via mill.

**Board Out:**
- −2 Authority of the Consuls *(no creatures entering on their side frequently)*
- −1 Lifecreed Duo *(small body, effect needs many triggers)*
- −1 Space-Time Anomaly *(they may have permission; save for when you're very high life)*

**Board In:**
- +2 Spell Snare *(counters their counterspells and 2-CMC interaction)*
- +2 Enter the Avatar State *(hexproof on Hope Estheim on their turn = untouchable)*

**Key notes:** Cavern of Souls naming "Angel" is your most important play. Aim to have a Cavern in play before resolving Giada or Lyra. Enter the Avatar State for {W} on their end step gives Hope Estheim hexproof, flying, lifelink, first strike — makes him very hard to answer.

---

### vs. Reanimator / Graveyard Decks

**Plan:** Counter their key reanimation spells (No More Lies + Syncopate exile), exile their targets with Prayer of Binding.

**Board Out:**
- −2 Angel of Vitality *(life gain amplification less critical when opponent is racing on combo)*
- −1 Lifecreed Duo *(passive gain not fast enough for this matchup)*
- −1 Leyline of Hope *(conditional; 4 mana is expensive when you need interaction)*
- −1 Space-Time Anomaly *(too slow)*

**Board In:**
- +3 Prayer of Binding *(flash exile of graveyard targets or reanimated creatures; gains 2 life)*
- +2 Temporal Cleansing *(bounce their reanimated finisher)*

**Key notes:** No More Lies is your best tool — it exiles the countered spell rather than putting it in the graveyard. Keep Syncopate mana available at all times. Prayer of Binding at flash can exile a reanimated creature after it enters, before it attacks.

---

### vs. Midrange (Jund/Golgari/Rakdos)

**Plan:** Out-value on life gain, force them to trade down, grind to a Space-Time Anomaly kill.

**Board Out:**
- −2 Syncopate *(their threats are cheap; you need to be proactive)*
- −2 Sheltered by Ghosts *(they have removal that can break the aura)*

**Board In:**
- +2 Vanguard Seraph *(surveil 1 on each life gain event finds your finishers faster)*
- +2 Case of the Uneaten Feast *(additional lifegain trigger per creature ETB; graveyard recursion when solved = massive late-game advantage)*

**Key notes:** The midrange matchup is typically your best. They have individual card quality but not the explosive starts or the interaction density to stop your life gain engine. Focus on establishing multiple Hope Estheim triggers per turn.

---

## General Sideboarding Principles

1. **Never board out all 4 copies of No More Lies.** It's the backbone of the permission package.
2. **Keep Cavern of Souls against any deck with counterspells.** It's too valuable to trim.
3. **Space-Time Anomaly comes out against decks faster than turn 6.** It stays in against slower decks where your life total will be 35+.
4. **Fumigate is a keep-all-copies vs. creature decks.** The life gain off a 5-creature sweep is often 15-20 life — a massive stabilization.
5. **Enter the Avatar State is best as a combat trick or counterspell-dodge** on your key threat's turn. Flash it in response to a removal spell targeting Hope Estheim.
